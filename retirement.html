Retirement – it’s what they’ve saved for.

Our retirement solutions are designed to allow your employees to make the most of their pension savings and to take the full advantage of the pension freedoms.

As employees look toward you for guidance, we will be in support.

We can guide and support employees throughout their entire savings journey, close to and at retirement, helping them to better understand their options and supporting them as they select which will be the most beneficial. 

Employees can access their money in a way that is best suited to them through a seamless move to our flagship drawdown product, Active Money Personal Pension (AMPP).

And it’s more.

Standard Life offer the highest quality support to your people, through an interactive drawdown journey, online tools and calculators, triggered communications, and telephone support.

  • We are one of the largest drawdown providers in the UK
