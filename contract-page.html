Choosing a contract based solution will offer your
employees greater investment choice and flexibility
and will significantly reduce the governance burden
and administrative costs for you by managing all
aspects of your scheme.

Our contract based pension solution combines the
Group Flexible Retirement Plan (GFRP) and the
Group Self Invested Personal Pension (GSIPP) to
offer the complete package.


GFRP at a glance, your benefits:

  • You will be able to easily manage all the
    aspects related to your pension scheme
    through your own dedicated website.
  • We will support you to drive employee
    engagement; so that they value the workplace
    pension you provide and feel positive about
    their future finances.
  • You will enjoy market-leading features and
    benefits with our GFRP, rated 5 Star by
    Defaqto in 2016.
Your employees’ benefits:

  • More control and flexibility around their
    investment choices.
  • Allows your employees to save in a tax-efficient
    way.
  • Option to easily switch over to our GSIPP if they
    are looking for an even wider range of investment
    opportunities (subject to extra charges)
  • A dedicated website which will enable them to
    easily view and manage their individual pension
    plan online and through our mobile app
  • A fully portable scheme - GFRP won’t end if they
    later change jobs – employees can take their
    pension with them.

An industry first

In 2006, we become the first company to
offer capped drawdown within a workplace
pension, through our GFRP and GSIPP plans.

An award-winning service
Standard Life was named Best Group Pension
Provider three years in a row at the Corporate
Adviser Awards in 2016, 2015 and 2014.

GSIPP at a glance

Wide Investment choice, this is the main benefit
of our Group Self Invested Personal Pension.
Standard Life’s GSIPP opens the door to around
2,000 pension and mutual funds, plus a wide
variety of other investments ranging from
