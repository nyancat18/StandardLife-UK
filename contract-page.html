WE’LL DO THE HEAVY LIFTING

Choosing a contract based solution will offer your employees greater investment choices whilst significantly reducing your governance burden and administrative costs by allowing you to manage all aspects of your scheme.    Our contract based pension solution combines the Group Flexible Retirement Plan (GFRP) and the Group Self Invested Personal Pension (GSIPP) to offer the complete package.

GFRP at a glance
Your benefits:

  • You will be able to easily manage all aspects related to your pension scheme through your own dedicated and personalised website 
  • We will support you to drive employee engagement; so that they value their workplace pension and feel positive about their future finances.
  • You will enjoy market-leading features and benefits with our GFRP, rated 5 Star by Defaqto in 2016.

Your employees’ benefits:

  • More control and flexibility around their investment choices 
  • Allows your employees to save in a tax-efficient way
  • Option to easily switch over to our GSIPP if they are looking for an even wider range of investment opportunities (subject to extra charges).
  • A dedicated website which will enable them to easily view and manage their individual pension plan online and through our mobile app.
  • A fully portable scheme - GFRP won’t end if they later change jobs – employees can take their pension with them.

GSIPP at a glance
Wide Investment choice, this is the main benefit of our Group Self Invested Personal Pension. Standard Life’s GSIPP opens the door to around 2,000 pension and mutual funds, plus a wide variety of other investments, from government securities to gold bullion.

An industry first
In 2006, we become the first company to offer capped drawdown within a workplace pension, through our GFRP and GSIPP plans.

An award-winning service
Standard Life was named Best Group Pension Provider three years in a row at the Corporate Adviser Awards in 2016, 2015 and 2014. Contact us to find out more about our contract based solution.
