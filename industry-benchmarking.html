Large businesses, like yours, face global competition for talent. To attract the best in sector, employers can’t afford to get left behind. While offering a competitive benefits package is the key focus, competitive pressures can also mean a relentless search for efficiency.

Industry insight and knowing how competitive your pension benefit is comparing to your industry peers can highlight areas for potential improvement. It can help you to identify new ways to attract and retain key employees and to achieve better efficiencies.

If it’s important to you, it’s important to us. And we want to help. That is why we took a snapshot of our largest clients pension benefits offering, to help you evaluate how your benefit programmes compare with your sector.
