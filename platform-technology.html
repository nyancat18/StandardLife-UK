A highly configurable platform technology, designed to meet the complex requirements of large organisations like yours.

We have developed our own in-house technology, named Lifelines, as part of a fully integrated, branded and bespoke reward portal for you and your employees. This platform provides you with a dynamic and engaging experience for your employees, leaving little to be administered.

Our highly robust, automated, intuitive and secure platform - along with our years of  expertise and insight - will drive employee engagement, automate processes and support your business needs.

For your employees:

  • Holistic view of their total reward package by collating everything in one place, improving the perceived value of their benefits.
  • Easily view and manage online all of the aspects of their reward package, in one place and accessible through single sign on. 
  • Fully personalised view and access to the savings and benefits that are relevant to them.
  • Streamlined online journey due to high level integration
  • Access to a complete suite of modelling tools designed to help them make the right decision and plan for their short, medium and long term financial futures.

For your business

  • Providing from one source the key real-time management information your business needs to help shape your future direction and strategies.
  • Reducing the administrative burden and streamlining processes by integrating your workplace pension, savings and benefits with your HR and payroll systems.
  • Driving cost efficiencies by reducing the resource needed for administrative tasks and through a better benefit uptake (National Insurance Savings)
  • Ability to segment your workforce for personalised and targeted communications to drive employee engagement
  • Reduced risks, increased transparency, process reliability, a single source of data and no needto integrate multiple external interfaces.

Our Lifelens solution is made up of a series of technology ‘modules’ - all integrated to provide a complete solution. These modules include; pension administration; pension communication (the employer’s microsite) and Lifelens benefits.

Benefits consultancy, Vebnet

Vebnet, our in-house consulting team is specialised in the use and design flexible benefits. Our primary focus is on benefits that will inspire and engage your employees.
We can provide you with benchmarking information as well as talking to you through all of the current flexible benefits trends: from anytime benefits and eldercare, through to financial well-being and behavioural marketing.

We support you with design implementation of your benefits in a simple, effective and engaging way to support employee take-up.
